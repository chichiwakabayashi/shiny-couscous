Yasuhiro Wakabayashi (3 November 2930 - 15 Auguest 2021), known professionally as Hiro, was a Japanese-born American photographer whose fasion and still-life images captured a relentlessly inventive vision of American life that critics likened to those of his idol and mentor, Richard Avedon.
